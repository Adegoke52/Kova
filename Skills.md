# Kova Skills & Capabilities

## Core Functional Skills

### 1. WhatsApp-Triggered Invoicing (P0)
- **Capability:** Generates professional invoices and job summaries directly via WhatsApp messages.
- **Workflow:** Prompts for client name, services, and amount.
- **Speed:** Completion of the entire flow in under 30 seconds.
- **Intelligence:** Auto-assigns invoice numbers and drafts ready-to-send WhatsApp messages for the owner.

### 2. Branded Client Page Generation (P0)
- **Capability:** Automatically creates a custom, fast-loading (SSR) web page for each invoice.
- **Assets:** Includes business logo/badge, itemized job summary, job status, and payment status.
- **Optimization:** Optimized for 2-second load times on 3G connections and low-spec Android devices.

### 3. Real-Time Engagement Notifications (P0)
- **Capability:** Monitors client activity and alerts the business owner instantly.
- **Events:** Sends WhatsApp notifications when a client opens an invoice page or completes a payment.
- **Retention:** Acts as a primary "dopamine loop" to keep business owners engaged.

### 4. Integrated Payment Links (P0)
- **Capability:** Embeds one-click payment buttons on client pages.
- **Providers:** Integrates with local payment gateways (e.g., Paystack, Flutterwave).
- **Tracking:** Supports multi-state status tracking (Unpaid, Payment Claimed, Paid).

### 5. Reputation & Trust Building (P1)
- **Capability:** Automates the collection of client reviews via one-tap buttons on invoice pages.
- **Social Proof:** Accumulates transaction-backed ratings to build a business's "reputation lock-in."
- **Identity:** Auto-generates professional logo badges for businesses without existing branding.

## Management & Administrative Skills

### 6. Business Dashboarding (P1)
- **Capability:** Provides a mobile-optimized view of business performance.
- **Insights:** Tracks total earnings, invoice history, client records, and average ratings.
- **Data Lock-in:** Serves as the primary system of record for the business.

### 7. Smart Follow-ups (P2)
- **Capability:** Identifies overdue invoices and triggers automated or "one-tap" payment reminders.
- **Context:** Uses "Warm," "Formal," or "Direct" templates aligned with the business brand voice.

## Technical & Compliance Capabilities

### 8. PWA Architecture
- **Capability:** Operates as a Progressive Web App, ensuring an installable experience that works well on mobile.

### 9. Regulatory Compliance (Roadmap)
- **Capability:** Future-proofed for the FIRS e-invoicing mandate in Nigeria.
- **Standards:** Implementation of Peppol BIS Billing 3.0 structured data standards.

### 10. AES-256 Security
- **Capability:** Ensures all business and client data is encrypted at rest and in transit (TLS 1.3), adhering to NDPA (Nigeria Data Protection Act) standards.
